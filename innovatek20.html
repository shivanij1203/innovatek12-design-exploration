About IK20

Connection starts before students step on campus.

Disconnected and disaffected students often leave college within the first month of matriculation. What if we could reverse this trend by creating a more purposeful, connected experience for students before they even step onto campus?

InnovateK20 is devoted to helping college communities systematically integrate student voice into the college experience — before, during, and after students officially arrive.

From Early Learning Centers to Graduate Schools.

The recruitment and retention of faculty and staff is equally important. With a focused commitment to building authentic community, we work with educational institutions to ensure they are aligned with the sentiments and needs of their staff, professors, and adjunct faculty.
